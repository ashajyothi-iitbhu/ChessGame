The chessboard is divided into ranks (numbers) and files (letters). There are eight of each, and each is comprised of
eight squares of equal size.
The numbers 1 to 8 indicate ranks, and the letters a to g indicate files.

The uppercase letters denote the white pieces, while the lowercase letters denote the black pieces on the Chess Board.
